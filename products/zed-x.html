Deploy advanced perception today

The new ZED X is an IP67-rated Global Shutter camera built for robots, powered by Neural Depth Engine 2. With a robust aluminum enclosure, high-performance IMU, secure GMSL2 connection and external frame sync, it's ready for field operation.

100x greater detail than LiDAR

With dual 1920x1200 global shutter color sensors, the camera produces crystal-clear images that quickly capture any action-filled environment.

Secure GMSL2 connection

ZED X’s GMSL2 output is ideal for robotics. Transmit video without EMI through a rugged interface to a Jetson Orin over a distance of up to 15m with low latency.

Multi-camera synchronization

Get hardware synchronization for multiple connected devices, at frame-level within 100 microseconds. Giving you a total view of an environment, synchronization captures and aligns multiple RGB and depth images.

Industrial-grade enclosure

The IP67 rugged enclosure is perfect for agricultural and industrial environments. It ensures protection from dust and water ingress, a necessity for mobile robots.

High-performance IMU

The all-new IMU combines a 16-bit triaxial accelerometer and gyroscope with vibration resistance, ultra low noise and bias for exceptional motion tracking.

Neural Depth Engine 2

Built by us, Neural Depth Engine 2 allows +50% more accurate depth maps with robustness to untextured surfaces and lighting conditions, from 10 cm to tens of meters.

Built tough. For any environment.

The ZED X is designed for field operation. Its IP67 enclosure and GMSL2 is resistant to dust and water ingress, ensuring 24-7 operations, without interruptions. By providing a continual view of an environment, ZED X enables robots to operate in unstructured environments, and unpredictable conditions.

Designed for Superbots

Robots are machines that need to sense, decide, and act. ZED X equips your autonomous vehicle with the necessary tools for successful deployment.

Unrivaled lens quality

With its 9-elements glass lens, high MTF and optical distortion correction, the new ZED X optics are the most advanced lenses ever used in a stereo camera.

Choose between a 2mm lens for a wide field of view or a 4mm lens for increased depth and image quality at long range.

Powered by the most advanced vision software stack

The ZED X stereo camera uses Neural Depth Engine 2, the world's most advanced stereo depth sensing technology that virtually replicates the physical world.

Localization

High precision 3D positioning can be achieved by fusing localization data from several ZED X cameras and global navigation satellite systems (GNSS) using the latest ZED SDK.

Object detection

With the ZED SDK, combine depth and visual odometry to build an occupancy map of the robot environment, allowing for safe navigation, in both indoor and outdoor unstructured environments.

Multi-Camera Synchronization and Fusion

Connect up to 4x ZED X cameras on Jetson AGX Orin using hardware synchronization. With the ZED SDK, fuse point clouds and odometry from multiple cameras into a single source of data.

Frequently Asked Questions

The ZED X and ZED X Mini are shipped within 2-3 days and the ZED X One will be available in March 2024.

The ZED X must be connected to an NVIDIA Jetson system, which requires a specific capture card to deserialize the video signal coming from the camera. We provide our own capture card and will soon announce a list of compatible capture cards from other vendors.

The ZED X camera is compatible with the new NVIDIA Jetson Orin platform (developer kit only for now.)

Yes, we will provide drivers for 2-camera and 4-camera configurations. For any other configurations, we will provide support documentation.

Yes, the ZED SDK is compatible with the ZED X camera. If you have already developed an application for a ZED, ZED 2 or ZED 2i camera, it will be still working with the ZED X with some minor modifications that we will soon share in our documentation.

Your choice should be based on the minimum depth range required for your application. If depth sensing is necessary below 50cm, you should select the ZED X Mini.
