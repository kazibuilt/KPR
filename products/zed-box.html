Yes, you can power your ZED Box via Ethernet using a Power Source Equipment (PSE), usually a PoE+ switch. The PoE+ switch can deliver up to 30W (usable 25.5W) to power connected devices, called Powered Devices (PD). This means you only require a single Ethernet cable to both power the ZED Box and transfer data. This becomes especially convenient when setting up cameras and ZED Boxes in environments that are challenging to access with no traditional power outlets. Utilizing PoE+ not only streamlines the installation process but also reduces cable clutter, making for a cleaner and more efficient setup.
